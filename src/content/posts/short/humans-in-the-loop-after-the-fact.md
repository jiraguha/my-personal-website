Today, systems increasingly:

- Execute faster than anyone can review
- Act at scales humans cannot reason about
- Produce explanations that cannot be falsified

And yet humans are:

- "In the loop" only **after** execution
- Asked to justify decisions they did not make
- Unable to contradict the system meaningfully

This asymmetry isn't new in principle — but it has never existed at this scale. When the system moves faster than oversight, **accountability becomes theater**.
